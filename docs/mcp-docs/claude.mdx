Connect Smokeball to Claude as a custom connector. These steps apply to both [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) in the browser and the Claude Desktop app.

## Prerequisites

- A Claude plan that supports custom connectors {/* TODO: Confirm minimum Claude plan requirements at launch */}
- An active Smokeball account

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open connector settings">
    In Claude, go to **Settings → Connectors**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a custom connector">
    Click **Add custom connector** and enter:

    - **Name**: `Smokeball`
    - **URL**: `https://mcp.smokeball.com/mcp`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Smokeball">
    Claude opens a browser window asking you to sign in to your Smokeball account and authorise the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the connector in a chat">
    In a new chat, open the tools menu (the sliders icon) and make sure the Smokeball connector is enabled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify it works

Ask Claude something that uses your Smokeball data:

```text
What are my open matters?
```

Claude should call a Smokeball tool and show you the result.
