The Smokeball MCP server lets you connect an AI assistant to your Smokeball account, so you can work with your firm's data using natural language. **ChatGPT** and **Claude** are currently supported, with more clients planned.

## What is MCP?

The [Model Context Protocol (MCP)](https://modelcontextprotocol.io) is an open standard that lets AI assistants securely connect to external services like Smokeball. Once connected, your AI assistant can look up and act on your Smokeball data on your behalf, with no copying and pasting between apps.

## What can you do with it?

Once connected, you can ask your AI assistant things like:

- "Summarise the *Smith v. Jones* matter. Who's involved and what's outstanding?"
- "Find the contact details for opposing counsel on the Smith matter"
- "Find the signed retainer agreement on the Smith matter"
- "Draft a letter to the client with an update on settlement negotiations"
- "Add a task to the Smith matter to follow up with the expert next week"

Your assistant can search matters, contacts, and files, read and analyse documents, draft correspondence, and (with your confirmation) create matters and contacts or add tasks, memos, and calendar events. See the full list of [available tools](/docs/mcp-docs/tools).

Your assistant can only access the data your Smokeball user account can access. See [Security & Permissions](/docs/mcp-docs/security) for details.
